TechFest 2025

Get ready for the most exciting event of the year! Elite Technical College is proud to host TechFest 2025, our national-level hackathon and innovation summit, happening this April 15th-16th.

Event Highlights

  • 24-Hour Hackathon: Solve real-world challenges and compete for amazing prizes. Themes include AI for Good, Sustainable Tech, and Fintech.
  • Innovation Summit: Keynote speeches from industry leaders at Google, Microsoft, and Tesla.
  • Robotics Competition: Witness autonomous bots battle it out in the Robo-Wars arena.
  • Project Expo: A showcase of the brightest ideas and projects from students across the country.
  • Workshops: Hands-on sessions on Quantum Computing, Blockchain, and AR/VR development.

Registration

Registration is free for all students with a valid college ID. Team participation is encouraged for the hackathon. Registrations open March 20th.
