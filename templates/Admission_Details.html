Admissions 2025 Open

Online registration for the 2025 academic year starts from March 10th, 2025. We invite all aspiring students to apply early and secure their seat at one of the region's premier technical institutions.

How to Apply

  1. Visit the official admissions portal on our website.

  5. Submit your application and save the confirmation receipt.
