About the Diploma Program

Our Diploma programs are career-focused, three-year technical courses designed for quick entry into the industry. They offer a strong blend of theoretical knowledge and practical, hands-on training, ensuring students are job-ready immediately after graduation. Diplomas also provide an excellent pathway for direct lateral entry into B.Tech programs.

Available Diploma Branches

Computer Science (CS)

Focus on foundational programming, operating systems, and computer hardware.

Machine Learning (ML)

Introduction to data analysis, basic algorithms, and AI concepts for practical application.

Electronics & Comm. (EC)

Gain practical skills in electronic circuit design, testing, and communication systems.

Information Technology (IT)

Training in network administration, cloud basics, and system maintenance.

Agriculture

Technical expertise in modern farming methods, machinery, and crop management.
